    • Meter build

    Building super meter is crucial, especially in tough matchups. You should use TAP and charging moves whenever possible.

    Frametraps

    Standing - 5MK > 2MK
    Crouch - 2MK > 2MK or 5HP or 2HK
    - 2MP > 2MK
    - 2LP, 2LP, 2LP > 2MP
    - 2LP, 2LP > Headbutt LP
    - Dash low > Headbutt LP
    Jump-In - j.MP > 2HK
    TAP - TAP lvl 3+ > Super or Headbutt

    Safe jumps

    ⚠️ You can't safe jump against Ken's HP Shoryuken.

    Midscreen

    Dash low > 2LP > j.HP
    2HK > 2LP > j.HP
    Headbutt > Upper > 2LP > j.HP
    Ground Upper > TAP lvl1 > j.HP

    Corner

    Dash low > j.HP
    Headbutt > 2LP > j.HP
    Ground Upper > 2MK > j.HP

    During throw (midscreen)

    Hold (9) > jHP (Boxer, Feilong)
    Hold (9) > jLK (Cammy)
    ✅ Try learning the jump timing without using those setups to be less predictable.

    Ground Upper

    At mid and close range, use Ground Upper LK to quickly react to opponents walking forward.

    Ground Upper - Throw setup

    2MP > 2LP > Ground Upper > Throw
    Ground Upper whiffs on crouch, but leaves you close enough for MP throw. Mix with headbutt to counter throw attempt.

    Meter build post super

    Midscreen: Upper > Headbutt
    Corner: Headbutt

    Side switch

    - headbutt anti-air > dash low > headbutt HP
    - headbutt > upper > headbutt HP

    Autocorrect Dashes

    while holding your down charge, just hit one button (like HK here) at the moment they go behind you. Useful against Claw.

    Auto-Stand Bug

    A bug makes 2LP act as 5LP. Whiff 5LP to store the bug, then chain 2LK into 2LP.
